ACME Co. Cacti Harvesting Process

ACME Co. harvests premium wild cacti from the deserts of the world. They wanted to track where their cacti was coming from, and what quality of cacti they are collecting. ACME uses three patented methods of finding cacti, codenamed after local desert wildlife - "lizards", "jerboas", or "burros".


ACME wants to toggle their data in two ways: by which subset of cacti they're interested in (round, oblong, or succulent-shaped cacti), and also the defect rate on various important attributes.
